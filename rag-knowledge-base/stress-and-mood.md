# Stress And Mood Support

Source note: team-curated educational summary for a student wellness app.

This app provides general wellness education and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

Stress can affect sleep, concentration, exercise routines, and mood. A simple first step is to notice patterns: what days feel harder, whether sleep changes, and whether movement or social support helps.

Small recovery habits can help during busy weeks. Examples include a short walk, five minutes of breathing, writing down the next three tasks, taking breaks between study sessions, or speaking with a trusted person.

Low mood scores over several days should be treated with care. The app can suggest supportive habits, but it should not diagnose mental health conditions. Users who feel unsafe, overwhelmed, or persistently distressed should seek professional or emergency support.
