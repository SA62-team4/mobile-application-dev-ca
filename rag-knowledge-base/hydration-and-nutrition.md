# Hydration And Nutrition Habits

Source note: team-curated educational summary for a student wellness app.

This app provides general wellness education and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

Basic hydration and regular meals can support energy and concentration. Users who forget to drink water can pair hydration with existing routines, such as drinking water after waking, before class, or after exercise.

Balanced meals do not need to be perfect. A practical plate might include a source of protein, a carbohydrate, vegetables or fruit, and water. During stressful periods, regular meals can be more useful than restrictive goals.

The app should avoid prescribing diets. It can encourage simple habits and suggest seeking professional guidance for medical nutrition needs.
