# Healthy Habits FAQ

Source note: team-curated educational summary for a student wellness app.

This app provides general wellness education and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

## How do I start if my routine is inconsistent?

Choose one habit that is easy enough to repeat. Examples include recording sleep each morning, walking for ten minutes, or setting a reminder to wind down before bed.

## How often should I review my wellness logs?

A short weekly review can help users notice patterns without overthinking every day. Look for trends in sleep, movement, and mood rather than judging one record.

## What if my data is sparse?

Sparse data means recommendations should be cautious. The first goal is consistent tracking, because better records make recommendations more personal.

## Can the chatbot answer medical questions?

The chatbot should provide general wellness education only. It should not diagnose, prescribe treatment, or replace professional support.
